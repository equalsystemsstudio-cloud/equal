Equal Play Store Assets Delivery

This folder contains the source files and ready exports for your Google Play listing.

Included:
1) store_feature_graphic.svg — Editable feature graphic (1024×500 design in SVG)
2) app_icon.svg — Editable app icon (source)
3) app_icon.png — PNG export (check size is 512×512; if not, export from SVG)
4) equal_logo.svg — Logo source
5) play_store_listing.txt — Final/working draft of listing copy

Where to upload in Play Console:
- App icon: 512×512 PNG (Play Console > Main Store Listing > Graphics)
- Feature graphic: 1024×500 JPG or PNG (Graphics)
- Screenshots: 1080×1920 or similar (Phone), plus any tablet sizes you choose
- Privacy Policy URL: https://equal-privacy.vercel.app/privacy-policy
- Contact Email: support@equal-co.com

Editing/Exporting tips:
- SVG sources are fully editable in Figma/Illustrator/Inkscape.
- To export PNGs from SVG: open in Inkscape/Illustrator/Figma and export at exact pixel sizes.
- If you prefer, I can export and add the final PNG/JPG files to this folder.

Delivery:
- Everything in this folder is zipped to equal-play-assets.zip for easy sharing.
